## TL;DR

TechPulse is live. Every day, our AI pipeline scans the web for the most relevant tech and gaming stories, writes a full professional article, and publishes it here automatically — no human required.

## What Is TechPulse?

TechPulse is a fully automated tech journalism platform built on a zero-cost, high-performance stack. Powered by the Gemini AI API and fueled by real-time news data from NewsAPI, the system wakes up every morning, identifies the most trending story in Gaming, AR, VR, or Technology, and produces a complete, SEO-optimized article.

No editors. No delays. Just signal.

## The Tech Stack

| Layer | Technology | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Discovery | NewsAPI | Trending story detection |
| Intelligence | Gemini 1.5 Flash | Article generation |
| Hosting | GitHub Pages | Free, fast static hosting |
| Automation | GitHub Actions | Daily scheduled publishing |
| Site Engine | Hugo | Static site generation |

## Coverage Areas

We cover the four pillars of next-gen tech:

- **Gaming** — hardware launches, game releases, esports, industry moves
- **Augmented Reality** — AR glasses, spatial computing, enterprise AR
- **Virtual Reality** — headsets, VR platforms, immersive experiences
- **Technology** — AI, semiconductors, consumer tech, industry analysis
