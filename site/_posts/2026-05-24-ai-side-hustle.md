The story of two software engineers creating an inbox cleaning app as a side hustle and selling it for $4 million is a fascinating one. The app, which utilized machine learning algorithms to clean and organize inboxes, quickly gained popularity, attracting the attention of potential buyers. After a successful acquisition, the engineers sold the app for $4 million, a remarkable achievement considering they did not have to quit their day jobs to pursue the venture.
